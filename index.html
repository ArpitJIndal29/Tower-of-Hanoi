Tower of Hanoi

Aim:

To move all the discs to some second or third pillar and place them in same sequence as in start

Rules:

You cannot place a disc of large size on a disc of small size.

How to Play:

Click on a pillar to lift the topmost disc on the pillar then click on the pillar on which you want to place the disc.
